Residential

SMS Glass Ltd. offers full-service residential glass repair. Because a window consists of more than just glass, we have developed an extensive network of suppliers. This helps us to ensure that we can handle any repair or servicing that may be required. Our knowledgeable staff can address your questions and in most circumstances provide a comprehensive estimate over the phone. All double glazed window panels carry a 5-year manufacturer limited warranty from respected brands with an option for a 10-year warranty available. Call SMS Glass for terms and conditions on the limited warranty. Our onsite service vehicles are kept well-stocked for standard repairs and servicing, and are backed by our parts department and a network of suppliers. Our onsite services are available 6 days a week.

Commercial

At SMS Glass, our staff are experienced in all types of glazing systems, from high-rise technical glazing to storefronts and entries. Our technicians are trained and certified on suspended platforms, Boson’s chair, and scissor lifts/boom lifts. With well-equipped service vehicles and the ability to facilitate 24-hour emergency calls, SMS Glass can solve all your glazing needs.
